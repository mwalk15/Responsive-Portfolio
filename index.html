About Me

I am a 27 year old optomistic person who sees all the good in the world and believes that fairytales can come true. I have a background in legal, dental, and medical. I am now beginning my transition into computer coding because after years of running from things I secretly loved but did not think I was capable of succeeding in, I decided to grab the bull by the horns and sink or swim.

One of my greatest accomplishments is the Northwestern University Coding Bootcamp. For the pace, it can be fairly difficult if you get behind, but as long as you keep at it both in and out f the classroom, you will be perfectly fine.The instructors and Teachers Aids are amazing and are determined to push you and see you succeed. Now I can say I am officially a developer! AHH just the sound of that is music to my tiny ears!
